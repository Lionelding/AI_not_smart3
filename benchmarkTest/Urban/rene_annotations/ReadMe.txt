################################################English################################################
Annotations of the René-Lévesque video (Polytrack format)
The video was annotated from frame 7200 to 8200. Pedestrians were not annotated.

File					Description
rene_gt.sqlite			The annotation of all bike and cars
rene_gt_bike.sqlite		The annotation of all bike only
rene_gt_cars.sqlite		The annotation of all cars only

Metrics tool must be use with ABSDIST 24 to compare with:
Jodoin, J.-P., Bilodeau, G.-A., Saunier, N., Urban Tracker: Multiple Object Tracking in Urban Mixed Traffic, Accepted for IEEE Winter conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV14), Steamboat Springs, Colorado, USA, March 24-26, 2014 
http://www.jpjodoin.com/urbantracker/
