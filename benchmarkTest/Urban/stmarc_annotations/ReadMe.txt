################################################English################################################
Annotations of the St-Marc video (Polytrack format)
The video was annotated from frame 1000 to 1999
File								Description
stmarc_gt.sqlite					The annotation of all road users in the video defined by a a mask.
stmarc_gt_bike.sqlite				The annotation of all bike in the video defined by a a mask.
stmarc_gt_cars.sqlite				The annotation of all cars in the video defined by a a mask.
stmarc_gt_pedestrians.sqlite		The annotation of all pedestrians in the video defined by a a mask.

Metrics tool must be use with ABSDIST 113 to compare with:
Jodoin, J.-P., Bilodeau, G.-A., Saunier, N., Urban Tracker: Multiple Object Tracking in Urban Mixed Traffic, Accepted for IEEE Winter conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV14), Steamboat Springs, Colorado, USA, March 24-26, 2014 
http://www.jpjodoin.com/urbantracker/
